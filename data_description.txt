* Background Information *
- Subreddit -> Subreddit hyperlink network
- Hyperlink: originates from post in "source community," links to set in "target community"
- Each hyperlink has three pieces of data: timestamp, sentiment of "source community," text property vector

* Statistics *
- 55,863 nodes (subreddits)
- 858,490 edges (hyperlinks)

* Qualities of Graph *
- Directed
- No Self-Edges
- Duplicated Edges OK
